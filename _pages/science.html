I am a scientist

My love of science stretches back to before I can remember. Starting with bug catching in the backyard, to deciding that cloning would be a great 5th grade science project, to beginning a Ph.D. program in biochemistry, science has been a driving force in my life.

Not only do I love to do science, I also love to share science. I take every opportunity I can to share my research at scientific conferences in front of a technical crowd. I also look for ways to make science more accessible to the average person. Doing so not only helps them understand what I am doing, but it also helps me make sure I myself understand what I am doing.
