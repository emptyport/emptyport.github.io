Entomology

I can't remember a time in my life when I wasn't collecting insects.

When I got to college, I started working in the Monte L. Bean Life Science Museum at BYU in the insect collection. I learned to properly pin an insect and quickly became the top pinner. Later on I would identify insects and arachnids from pitfall traps for a surveying project out at Dugway Proving Grounds.

I don't do as much as I'd like with entomology at the moment; I used to run a blog about bugs as a learning resource and as a place to showcase my insect photography, but then grad school started and I got pretty busy.
